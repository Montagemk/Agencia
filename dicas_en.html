Essential Tips for Organizing Your Freight or Move with Total Peace of Mind

Planning a freight or move might seem challenging, but with the right tips, everything becomes simpler and more organized! The Craft Profissionais team has prepared this practical guide to help you get organized, ensure the safety of your belongings, and have a stress-free experience.

1. Planning is Key!

  • Start Early: Set the moving/freight date as early as possible to have time to organize everything.
  • Make a Detailed Inventory: List all items to be transported. This not only helps with an accurate quote but also ensures nothing is forgotten.
  • Declutter Smartly: Take the opportunity to get rid of items you no longer use. Donating, selling, or discarding excess can reduce the volume and cost of transportation.

2. Smart and Secure Packing

  • Varied Box Sizes: Use large boxes for light items (bedding, pillows) and smaller boxes for heavy items (books, dishes).
  • Label Boxes: Write the main contents and destination room on each box (e.g., "Kitchen - Fragile," "Bedroom - Clothes"). This speeds up unloading and organization at the new location.
  • Extra Protection for Fragile Items: Use bubble wrap, crumpled newspapers, blankets, or towels to wrap delicate items like dishes, glass, and electronics.
  • Essential Bag: Prepare a bag with first-necessity items for the first few days (clothes, hygiene products, important documents, medications).

3. Preparing Appliances and Furniture

  • Unplug and Clean: Appliances like refrigerators, stoves, and washing machines must be unplugged, cleaned, and thoroughly dried before transport.
  • Disconnect and Empty: Make sure all cables, hoses, and connections are disconnected and that there are no perishable foods in refrigerators or freezers.
  • Professional Assembly and Disassembly: Don't worry about assembling or disassembling your furniture. Craft Profissionais has a specialized team to perform this service, ensuring your furniture arrives intact and is reassembled correctly.

4. On the Day of Freight/Move: What to Expect?

  • Be Present: It's important that you or someone you trust accompanies the loading and unloading process to answer questions and guide the team.
  • Check Items: Upon arrival at the destination, quickly check to confirm that all boxes and items have been delivered according to the inventory.
  • Relax and Enjoy: With proper planning and Craft Profissionais taking care of the transport, you can focus on enjoying your new space.
